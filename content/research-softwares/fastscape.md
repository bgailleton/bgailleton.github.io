Fastscape is a Landscape Evolution Modelling framework, I participated to some development and used it mostly during my postdoc at the GFZ Potsdam. This is my go-to tool for designing long-term Landscape evolution models or testing new processes.

## Ressources

Multiple publications and ressources:
- [the OG paper - BW2013](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0169555X12004618): describe the uynderlying graph and topological ordering method and SPL implicit solver
- [Implicit sediment-transport solver - YBG2019](https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1029/2018JF004867)
- [The original fasscape in FORTRAN + python](https://fastscape.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html)
- [Newer, fancier version in C++/python](https://fastscapelib.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)

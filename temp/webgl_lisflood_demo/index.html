WebGL1 LisFlood-like Shallow Water Demo

This is a minimal WebGL1 GPGPU demo inspired by a LisFlood-like explicit scheme. The DEM is loaded from dem.png (256×256 grayscale). Water depth and discharge are stored in float textures and updated each frame using fragment shaders.

Replace dem.png with your own 256×256 grayscale DEM and adjust zMin/zMax in demo.js to match your data range.

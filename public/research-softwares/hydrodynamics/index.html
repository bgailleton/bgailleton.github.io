Hydrodynamics

Hydrodynamics is the WebGL1 port of my Lisflood-style solver. It keeps the stencil math honest inside a shader while I experiment with boundary conditions and timesteps before promoting changes to heavier codes.

Solver mode Switching modes resets the computation.

Inject a single-pixel pulse in the basin center, then adjust the color scale to inspect thin overland flow versus deep pooling.

Sim time 0.0 s
100 m

The current setup loads the dem.png surface (10 m cells), integrates with dt = 1e-2 s, and exposes the inflow curtain along rows 180-186 so I can pulse floods directly in the browser. Cranking up the slider is a quick gut-check on how much head the basin can take before rolling a bore downstream.
